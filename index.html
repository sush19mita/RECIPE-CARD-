TIRAMISU RECIPE

30 minutes (plus 4‚Äì6 hours chilling in the fridge for best flavor and texture)

üçΩ Servings: 8

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 8 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfinger biscuits
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder (for dusting)
  1. In a saucepan, whisk egg yolks and sugar until well blended.
  2. Add milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils; boil gently for 1 minute.
  3. Remove from heat, cover, and chill in the fridge for 1 hour.
  4. Whip heavy cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  5. In a bowl, beat mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  6. Gently fold the chilled egg mixture into mascarpone, then fold in whipped cream.
  7. Combine coffee and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish.
  8. Quickly dip ladyfingers into coffee mixture and arrange in a single layer in a dish.
  9. Spread half the cream mixture over ladyfingers, then repeat layers.
  10. Dust with cocoa powder, cover, and chill for 4‚Äì6 hours before serving.
